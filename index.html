About CWC

CWC is an official Club declared in the Year 2020 amidst of Covid-19 pandemic. The club is organised by the ITER's placement department and the SOA's Aluminis. We come up with the ideas and opportunies for those students who are good at coding and for those who aren't. This club is basically meant for all those ITERians who are willing to grasp knowledge in the field of coding , aptitude/reasoning and all the placement stuffs.

Our Motto is just to Code , Code and Code.

In this current era , everything is going online ,it may be in any kind like apps, websites, researches,etc. We want eceryone to improve their coding and logical skills through our platform which will lead them to a bright future.

Regular Coding Quizzes

We daily conduct Quizzes or problems to solve for the interview preparation and logic building.

Open Contribution

We give a chance to eligible student for contributing questions/posts related to coding/programming.

Coding Competitions

We conduct Ranked Competitions on HackerRank, CodeChef.Questions are selected taking into consideration for the acedemic as well as competitive environment.

Certification

Those who contribute to the club with full sincerity and motivation, will receive a certificate after graduating from ITER , which will be approved from the placement cell.
